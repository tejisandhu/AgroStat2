About Us

Our Story

As BTech ECE students, we Tejbir Singh and Jatin Sharma were passionate about technology and its potential to make a real difference. During our final semester project, we saw a disconnect: limited information and outdated practices hindering the agricultural sector. This sparked AgroStat - a platform to empower farmers with knowledge and tools for success. Born from a final project, AgroStat has become a labor of love. We pour our time and expertise into researching, writing content, and developing functionalities. Every piece of information and every line of code reflects our dedication to creating a valuable resource for the agricultural community.

Agriculture Universities

Empowering informed decisions: Agriculture universities play a crucial role in developing the next generation of agricultural leaders. By leveraging cutting-edge research and education, these institutions cultivate a pipeline of talent equipped to tackle the challenges facing our food systems. This foundation, combined with the power of data analysis and prediction offered by our intelligence dashboard, empowers a future where agriculture is more sustainable, productive, and resilient.

Why AgroStat?

AgroStat is our project to show and empower those farmers who don't have access to good decision making choices due to lack of data driven insigts to make an informed decisions about their farms and their crops. In AgroStat there is also a feature included in to check a disease of a crop by just uploading the image of a leaf which could be very important in treating it accordingly without any misdiagnosing.
